Frequently Asked Questions

How much faster is 3D printing vs traditional tooling?

Typically 3-4 weeks vs 12-16 weeks for traditional mold making. Design changes cost nothing with 3D printing vs $5,000-20,000 for traditional mold modifications.

What hardness do you achieve?

17-4 PH reaches 50-52 HRC post-hardening, matching traditional forged tool steel. 100% of parts are hardness-tested for specification compliance.

Is the quality equivalent to traditional tooling?

Yes. 99.5% density DMLS steel equals forged material properties. Parts perform identically to traditional mold inserts in production molding.

Can you do design optimization?

Yes. Our DFM analysis is free with quotes. We recommend cooling channel placement, support strategies, and cost-saving design modifications.
